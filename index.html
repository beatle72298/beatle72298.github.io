About

Influences:
Pink Floyd, Pearl Jam, John Mayer

Sounds Like:
Alabama Shakes,

Joe Diliberto may sound like a continuation of the 70s, but he most certainly isn't old. Born and raised in the not-all-that-magic city of Birmingham, Alabama, Joe's loving single mother exposed him to her treasure trove of rock n' roll classics at an extremely early age. For this reason he claims to have almost been born in a, "generational sonic gap." This probably explains why he quickly became enamored with the six-string. At the ripe young age of 18, Joe ventured farther South to the sweet crescent piece of land known as New Orleans, LA. As an artist he has dedicated himself to revitalizing the spacious psychedelic sounds of British prog-rock group Pink Floyd.
